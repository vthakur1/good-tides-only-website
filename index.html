Good questions

The FAQ

⏰ What are check-in and check-out times?
Check-in is 4pm. Check-out is 10am. Early check-in and late check-out are occasionally available — ask Pam when you book. Your booking confirmation has the full details.
🔑 How do I get in?
Self check-in via keypad — 24 hours a day. Your entry code arrives before your stay. No key pickup, no waiting, no coordinating with anyone. Drive up, punch in, you're home.
🐾 Are dogs welcome?
Yes — two dogs, up to 50 lbs each. A $100 pet fee is charged at booking. Fort Morgan beach is dog-friendly year-round (leashes required). The dog will have an opinion about this trip. It will be a good one.
🏖 How far is the beach?
A two-minute walk via the neighborhood's beach access path. Six beach chairs and a wagon are provided — load up and go. The Gulf will still be there when you arrive.
🍳 What's in the kitchen?
Everything you need for a week of actual cooking — full appliances, island seating, dining table, coffee maker, blender, toaster, wine glasses, and the lobster pot. The kitchen island tends to become the social center of the property by day two. Plan accordingly.
🚗 Is there parking?
Yes — a covered 2-car carport under the house. No circling, no meters, no parking garage. You pull up, unload, and start your vacation. Immediately.
🏖 Are beach towels provided?
Bath towels and linens are provided. Beach towels are personal — pack yours. If you forget, Walmart in Gulf Shores is 20 minutes east on arrival day. Consider it your first Gulf Coast adventure.
📋 What is the cancellation policy?
Moderate cancellation policy — full refund if cancelled at least 30 days before check-in. Check your booking confirmation for the exact terms. Life happens; we get it.
